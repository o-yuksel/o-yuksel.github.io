In my master's thesis, titled “Game theoretical analysis of cooperation and cheating among lipase-producing Yarrowia lipolytica sub-cultures,” I constructed a high-resolution common goods game to model competition between yeast strains in fermentation environments. I am committed to expanding my knowledge through seminars, presentations, and collaborations, with a goal to elucidate the fundamental factors driving evolution and develop mathematical models that accurately capture these mechanisms
